Mimic - Investor Proposal

1. Executive Summary

Mimic is a high-concept psychological thriller designed to function as both a festival-caliber standalone short film and a proof of concept for a feature adaptation. By leveraging industry connections, a lean but effective production model, and a commercially viable concept, Mimic represents an exciting investment opportunity in a growing slate of films under Red Fox Features.

2. Red Fox's Competitive Edge

  • Industry Relationships – Our team includes high-level technicians from major film sets, with years of personal and professional relationships that emphasize long-term collaboration and credibility.
  • In-House Post-Production – We reduce costs while maintaining quality by handling editing, sound design, and color grading ourselves.
  • Business-Driven Approach – We balance artistic vision with market viability, ensuring our projects have paths to distribution and monetization.

4. Revenue Potential for Investors

Your investment in Mimic creates multiple revenue opportunities through our strategic approach to distribution and IP development:

Digital Streaming & Licensing

Horror/thriller streaming platforms pay licensing fees or share ad revenue. Target platforms include:

  • Dust (Sci-Fi/Horror)
  • Shudder (Horror/Thriller)
  • ALTER (Short horror films)
  • Crypt TV (Horror franchises)

Investors receive a percentage of licensing deals based on equity stake.

Festival Prizes & Award Money

Top-tier festivals offer cash prizes for award-winning shorts:

  • Sundance, Tribeca, SXSW cash awards
  • Genre festivals with monetary prizes
  • Increased exposure from festival wins

Prize money is shared among investors according to equity percentage.

Feature Adaptation & IP Rights

Long-term value through concept development:

  • Sale/option of rights to production companies
  • Development of feature-length version
  • Partnerships with studios or streaming services

A successful festival run significantly increases adaptation potential.

Why This Matters for Investors

  • Mimic's contained, high-concept horror/thriller format aligns perfectly with successful festival and streaming acquisition patterns
  • Early investors gain priority access to our future slate of projects if Mimic opens industry doors
  • This proven model has launched careers and franchises in the horror/thriller space, following the Blumhouse approach of high-concept, low-budget films like Upgrade ($5M→$17M+), The Invisible Man ($7M→$144M), and Creep (under $1M to cult success)
